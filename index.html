Stanley, Idaho

Gateway to Sawtooth Adventure

Fun Facts

  • Stanley is often the coldest place in the lower 48 states, with an average of more than 280 days below freezing.
  • The town population varies from 40 to 300 people depending on the season.
  • The area is home to a variety of wildlife, including Chinook and Sockeye salmon, which migrate more than 900 miles to spawn in the Salmon River and nearby lakes.
